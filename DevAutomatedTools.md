# Automated Tools

While ZAP shouldnt be seen as a 'point and shoot' tool, its clear that the automated tools are very important to our users, and also (in many cases) need updating.

The automated tools include:

  * Spider
  * Passive Scanner
  * Active Scanner
  * Forced Browse
  * Port scanner
